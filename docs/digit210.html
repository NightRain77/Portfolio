DIGIT 210: Large Scale Text Analysis

I took DIGIT 210 in Spring 2021 as a Sophomore. This class started off with a lot of review from DIGIT 110, and then got into some new topics later on. Such as, Xquery, SVG, Cytoscape, and a little bit of Python. Our main project for the class was a group project where we formed teams to analyze a collection of documents of our choosing and later on present our findings using a project site that we created together. My project group chose to focus on songs from Disney's Aladdin franchise, and our findings were pretty interesting.


We worked on most of the project together, and as we had previously worked on a similar project together, Jackie Chan and I took the lead and tried to help and teach our other group member as the semester progressed. This project involved a little bit of everything we covered in class as well as some topics from DIGIT 110.
