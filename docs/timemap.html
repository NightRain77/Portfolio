Timemapper Project

Timemapper was a project assigned to me for one of my DIGIT classes. I had to reserach a famous person and create a timeline of their life, then embed my timeline into my website. I chose to do a timeline for Josephine Baker. She was an American-born French entertainer, French Resistance agent, and civil rights activist. I think that she was a very interesting person who impacted the world with her wish for peace and equality.
